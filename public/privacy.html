Kid Games Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Kid Games is designed for simple educational play. The current app does not use third-party ads and is intended to avoid third-party analytics in the first iOS release.

What We Collect

  • On the web preview, a local test state may be stored in browser storage.
  • On iOS, future one-time purchase entitlement is intended to use Apple’s in-app purchase system.
  • The app is intended not to collect personal profile data for the core play experience.

Kids Category
