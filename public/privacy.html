Kid Games Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Kid Games is designed for simple educational play. The current app does not use third-party ads and is intended to avoid third-party analytics in the first iOS release.

What We Collect

  • On the web preview, premium unlock state may be stored in local browser storage.
  • On iOS, premium entitlement status is managed through Apple’s in-app purchase system.
  • The app is intended not to collect personal profile data for the core play experience.

Kids Category

Parent-facing actions such as purchases, restore purchases, support, and policy links are intended to sit behind a parental gate in the iOS app.

Contact

For support questions, use the support page linked from the app or App Store listing.
